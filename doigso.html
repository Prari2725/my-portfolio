Dalya Baron

Astrophysics Research Student.

  I am a graduate student at the Tel Aviv University Astrophysics Department, deeply passionate about understanding the universe through the lens of data and computation. My research focuses on active galactic nuclei (AGN), exploring their pivotal role in galaxy evolution and the broader dynamics of the cosmos. In addition to my astrophysics work, I specialize in developing and applying machine learning algorithms to astronomical datasets. This interdisciplinary approach allows me to uncover patterns and insights in complex, large-scale data, driving innovation in both astrophysics and data science. Beyond academia, I am enthusiastic about science communication and believe in making complex ideas accessible to all.
